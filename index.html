Sync

Sync is an energetic cyborg rabbit, he tries to act cool, but he always ends up letting his boundless enthusiasm get the better of him. Sync's favourite pastime is cooking, he enjoys making sure his loved ones are well nourished.

Ceres

Though frequently sleepy, Ceres is a strong and patient bear. Some find his presence intimidating, but the truth is he has a heart of gold. His hobby is gardening, Ceres loves to grow vegetables and herbs for Sync to cook with.
