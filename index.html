Sync

Sync is an energetic cyborg rabbit, he tries to act cool but always ends up letting his boundless enthusiasm get the better of him. His hobby is cooking.

Ceres

Though freqently sleepy, Ceres is a strong and patient bear. Some find his presence intimidating, but the truth is he has a heart of gold. His hobby is gardening.
